An Introduction to Blogs

A blog is an online journal, typically on a website. Including opinionated text, engaging images, creative videos, gifs and comments, blogs are a way to engage with the public or with a specific niche audience. They can be utilised by a singular person, or large companies and corporations. Beginning with the popularisation of the internet and computers, they have been around for a long time and will continue to polarise the masses with informal monologue.
